Art eating Art

4,377 views · 1 year ago

Coria Mocibi

Female figures such as muses are romanticized, aestheticized and serialized in favor of narratives of art history that often exclude the true stories of these women, many of them also artists, devising their reports of violence in the name of art.

The masculinization of content and the aestheticization of violence in high culture art or mass media are also themes that intervene in this work of video art.
